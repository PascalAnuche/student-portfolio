Projects

Things I’ve designed and built — small tools that solve real problems for students. Every one of them started as a complaint from a classmate.

CampusEats

A mobile-first menu site for the food vendors around my estate in Abuja. Classmates and neighbours can check each vendor’s daily menu and prices before stepping out — the vendors update a simple shared sheet, and the site reflects it. My first fully responsive build.

  • HTML5
  • CSS3
  • Flexbox

StudyCircle

A study-group scheduler for my online cohort at Miva. Create a group, pick a weekly virtual meeting slot on the timetable grid, and track who showed up. Group data persists in localStorage, and the clash-detection logic taught me more about arrays than any lecture did.

  • JavaScript
  • localStorage
  • CSS Grid

NairaWise

A pocket-money tracker built after I ran out of allowance in week two of the semester — twice. Log spending by category, see a monthly summary, and get a gentle warning when suya is eating your data-bundle budget. All vanilla JavaScript and DOM manipulation.

  • DOM
  • localStorage
