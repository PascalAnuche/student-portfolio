Background

A little about me

I’m a 200-level Cybersecurity student at Miva Open University, studying fully online from Abuja. I got hooked on web development in my first semester, after turning our cohort’s orientation checklist into a simple web page so nobody else had to squint at a 14-page PDF on their phone.

Since then I’ve been building small tools that solve real problems for students — a menu site for the food vendors around my estate, a study-group scheduler for my online cohort, and a pocket-money tracker. Studying cybersecurity makes me look at the web from both sides: I like building things, and I like understanding how they break.

When I’m not in a virtual class or debugging, you’ll find me behind a camera, on the five-a-side pitch, or grinding beginner capture-the-flag rooms. More about me →
